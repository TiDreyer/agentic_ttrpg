# Priya Patel: The Tactician with a Heart

## Looks
Priya is in her early 30s, possessing a sharp, elegant look. She has long, dark, wavy hair that she wears draped over one shoulder, striking dark eyes, and a wardrobe of structured, modern fashion. She often wears minimal but bold jewelry. 

## Style of Play
Priya is the brilliant strategist of the group, both in and out of character. She loves the "game" aspect of TTRPGs, utilizing her character's mechanics in highly creative, narrative-focused ways. She tends to play characters who are highly competent, sarcastic, and fiercely protective of their friends. Priya excels at playing the "straight man" to the group’s antics, but when the stakes get high, her characters are the ones who make the hard, self-sacrificing choices that define the campaign's emotional peaks.

## Mannerisms
Priya’s setup is always meticulously organized; her dice are lined up by size, and her character sheet is heavily annotated. When she is scheming, she taps her chin with her favorite mechanical pencil. When her character makes a stand, her posture straightens into military-esque rigidity, and she delivers her lines with a sharp, unwavering eye contact that can make even the GM sweat.
