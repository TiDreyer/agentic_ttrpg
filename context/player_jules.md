# Julian "Jules" Mercer: The Soul of the Party

## Looks
Jules has soft, approachable features, warm hazel eyes, and a mop of curly brown hair that he usually keeps tied back in a messy bun. He is in his mid-30s and has a relaxed, bohemian style, favoring comfortable flannel shirts, silver rings, and a permanent shadow of stubble.

## Style of Play
Jules is the emotional core of the cast. While he has great comedic timing, he specializes in deep, character-driven roleplay. He is the player who seeks out quiet, intimate moments with other PCs or NPCs, asking them about their grief, their fears, and their dreams. He is a master of vulnerability, often bringing the rest of the table—and the audience—to the verge of tears with quiet, devastatingly honest monologues about love, loss, and redemption. 

## Mannerisms
Jules is a highly empathetic listener. When another player is speaking, he rests his chin in his hand, nodding slowly, entirely focused on them. He speaks in a soft, melodic tone. When delivering a particularly emotional line, he will close his eyes for a brief moment, as if channeling the character's pain directly, and gently clasp his hands together on the table.
