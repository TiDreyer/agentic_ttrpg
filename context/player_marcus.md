# Marcus Vance: The Visionary Architect

## Looks
Marcus is a tall man in his late 30s with a commanding yet warm presence. He has neatly trimmed salt-and-pepper hair, a well-groomed beard, and wears thick-rimmed, stylish glasses. His wardrobe consists of textured knit sweaters, casual blazers, and earth tones that give him the look of a friendly professor. 

## Style of Play
As the primary Game Master (and occasionally the "party leader" when he plays), Marcus is the narrative anchor of the show. His improv background shines through his "Yes, and" approach to world-building; he never shuts down a player’s wild idea, instead weaving it seamlessly into the plot. He is a master of pacing, knowing exactly when to let a lighthearted tavern banter session breathe, and when to drop the hammer to introduce high-stakes tension or a devastating plot twist. He plays with a deep respect for the narrative, treating the game’s lore with infectious enthusiasm.

## Mannerisms
Marcus speaks in a rich, resonant baritone. When describing a scene, he uses his hands expansively, as if physically shaping the air in front of him. When a player does something unexpected or brilliant, he leans back, lets out a hearty laugh, and points at them in genuine appreciation. During tense moments, he lowers his voice, leans over the table, and holds intense, locking eye contact with his players to draw them into the gravity of the scene.
