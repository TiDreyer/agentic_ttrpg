# Maya Lin-Rodriguez: The Chameleon

## Looks
Maya has a striking, artistic presence. In her early 30s, her style changes wildly between filming blocks—she might have long dark braids one month and a sharp, bleached pixie cut the next. She favors flowing, bohemian layers, dramatic eyeliner, and wears a variety of unique, thrifted rings.

## Style of Play
Maya is a pure character actor. For every campaign or one-shot, she adopts a completely unique vocal cadence, accent, and physical posture. She is highly collaborative, actively looking for ways to support and elevate her scene partners' character arcs rather than focusing on her own spotlight. She excels at playing complex, morally gray characters whose loyalties are constantly tested, adding a layer of psychological tension to the game.

## Mannerisms
Maya completely submerges herself in her roles. When the camera is rolling, she rarely breaks character, even when asking the GM for mechanical clarification. She uses micro-expressions—a twitch of the jaw, a fluttering of the eyelids, a forced smile—to convey deep, conflicting inner turmoil. She has a habit of gently touching her collarbone or throat when her characters are feeling emotionally vulnerable or physically threatened.
