# Chloe Kim: The Spark Plug

## Looks
Chloe is in her late 20s, petite, and radiates infectious energy. She has a sharp, asymmetrical bob haircut—often dyed a subtle pastel color like lavender or rose gold—and expressive, almond-shaped eyes. She favors vibrant, vintage-inspired clothing, statement earrings, and is rarely seen without a thermos of tea.

## Style of Play
Chloe is the instigator of the group. She plays characters who are impulsive, curious, and fiercely loyal. She excels at physical comedy and fast-paced verbal banter, often being the one to poke the sleeping dragon or ask the suspicious NPC the one question they didn’t want to answer. However, Chloe is not a chaotic troll; her instigations are always designed to drive the plot forward and force the other characters to react, creating incredible dramatic friction.

## Mannerisms
Chloe is a blur of motion at the table. She fidgets with her dice constantly, gasps audibly at low rolls, and will literally jump in her chair when a plan succeeds. She is incredibly expressive; her face instantly telegraphs her character’s internal panic, smugness, or heartbreak. When her character is in a tight spot, she has a habit of biting her lower lip and rapidly tapping her fingers against her cheek.
